Security Review

a methodology for taking stock of your personal digital security

A Security Review is a structured walkthrough of the accounts, devices, and habits that make up your digital life — finding the places where a small change in convenience buys a meaningful change in safety.

The review covers password practice, two-factor authentication, email and account recovery, device security, banking and credit, scam awareness, and the household paperwork that holds it all together.
